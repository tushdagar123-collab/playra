Team Battle mode allows players to compete in teams during live quizzes. Each team earns points based on correct answers and response speed. Scores are updated in real-time on the leaderboard, making the competition interactive and engaging for classrooms, events, and multiplayer quiz sessions.

Login to your account and click on "Create Quiz" from your dashboard. You'll be taken to the quiz editor where you can add questions, set timers, choose quiz modes, and customize your session.

Free users can host up to 50 participants in Classic Mode. For larger audiences or unlimited participants, upgrading to a Pro plan is recommended.

Free users can create only 3 Team Battle quizzes per account. Once this limit is reached, you'll need to upgrade to a Pro plan to unlock unlimited Team Battle sessions.

You will see an upgrade message asking you to move to Premium. Your existing quizzes and data remain safe — you simply won't be able to create new quizzes until you upgrade or your quota resets.

Yes, live leaderboard is available in all quizzes — including Classic Mode, Team Battle, and Classroom Mode. Scores update in real-time so participants can see rankings as they answer.

Export reports are available for Premium users only. Premium users can export detailed performance analytics, including individual scores, response times, and question-by-question breakdowns.
