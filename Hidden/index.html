Features

Everything you need for
live engagement

Three powerful modes designed for every scenario — from intimate classrooms to packed auditoriums.

Live Quiz Mode

Real-time interaction at its finest. Launch quizzes that sync across hundreds of devices instantly. See live leaderboards, timed questions, and instant feedback — all with zero lag.

  • Real-time sync across devices
  • Live leaderboard & analytics
  • Custom timer per question

Team Battle Mode

Divide your audience into teams and ignite competition. Collaborative answering, team scores, and head‑to‑head rounds create unforgettable group experiences.

  • Auto or manual team assignment
  • Collaborative scoring system
  • Head-to-head battle rounds

Classroom Mode

Designed for educators. Control the pace, review answers together, and track individual student progress. Turn every lesson into an engaging, data‑driven experience.

  • Teacher-controlled pacing
  • Individual progress tracking
  • Exportable performance reports
